## Change 2 — trailing_lines 3-item compact

**Current behavior** (3-item trailing produces 3 slots):
- slot A: ("", sub("item 0"))
- slot B: (main("item 1"), "")
- slot C: ("", sub("item 2"))

**Required behavior when len(trailing_lines) == 3:**
- slot A: ("", sub("item 0"))
- slot B: (main("item 1"), sub("item 2"))   ← compact: items 1 and 2 paired on one slot

So instead of 3 slots, produce 2 slots. The compact-pair only fires when len == 3. All other lengths (0, 1, 2, 4+) keep current behavior unchanged.

**Why surgical:** None of the other 5 MB depos use len==3 trailing_lines. This branch will not affect Halprin, Williams, Butler, Blanks, or Olsen. Verify by re-running all 6 after the change.
