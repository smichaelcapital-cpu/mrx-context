KNOWN DEFECT FAMILIES (from HANDOFF_LOG.md):
1. Homophones witness restates (permit address → permanent address)
2. Homophones in ceremony language (your sworn → you're sworn)
3. Garbled acronyms (with and T → W&T)
4. Proper nouns split (lemon wood terrace → Lemonwood Terrace)
5. Proper noun lowercase (Warren seal → Warren Seal)
6. Compound words split (under paid → underpaid)
7. Self-corrections missing em-dashes (No no → No -- no)
8. Number format (25 years ago → Twenty-five years ago)

SCOPE (v0):
- Pull 20-30 known defects from Halprin only
- Spread across the depo (early, mid, late)
- Cover at least 5 of the 8 known categories
- Each row needs: location (page/line if available, or turn_idx),
  steno wrong version, MB final correct version, defect category,
  audio timestamp estimate (best available)

WHAT EACH ROW MUST HAVE:
- defect_id: HALPRIN_DEF_001, HALPRIN_DEF_002, etc.
- category: one of the 8 families above
- location: turn_idx OR page+line in MB FINAL
- steno_says: the wrong word/phrase verbatim from RAW
- final_says: the correct word/phrase verbatim from FINAL
- audio_timestamp_estimate: best guess in mm:ss format
  (if no good estimate, mark UNKNOWN — do not invent)
- difficulty_hypothesis: EASY / MEDIUM / HARD
  (EASY = clear pronunciation, MEDIUM = some noise, HARD = mumble or
  cross-talk or accent)

AUDIO TIMESTAMP STRATEGY:
We don't have per-stroke sync (B1 proved that). Estimate using:
- Position in transcript (turn N of total turns) × audio duration
- Rough mapping: 340 minutes / total_turns_in_halprin ≈ minutes_per_turn
- This is approximate, NOT precise. Whisper will return its own
  timestamps when run. We use estimates only to find the right
  section to verify.
